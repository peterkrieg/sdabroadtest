Our Program

To advise and facilitate families hoping to give their child an immersive experience in western culture and a summer of fun. Summer Destinations LLC is a consulting service that matches Chinese youth, ages 8-17, with summer camps in the United States. Our Directors have years of hands-on experience attending and working at the top summer camps in the residential summer camp world. This service is free to our campers and families. All we ask for is a referral after a successful and positive experience.

Family Meeting

We arrange a personal meeting with the potential camper and their family. At this meeting our local director will not only explain the process and the camping experience in detail, but also identify the needs and wants of both the prospective camper and his/her parents.
