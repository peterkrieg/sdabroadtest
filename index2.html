What We Do

Summer Destinations LLC is a consulting service that matches Chinese youth, ages 6-15, with partner camps and programs in the United States. Our Directors have years of hands-on experience attending and working at the top summer camps in the residential summer camp world. With our decades of top tier summer camp experience and relationships, Summer Destinations LLC, provides an unmatched service that focuses on personal development, and cross-cultural exchange, and preparation for future study abroad.
